Wordle

The Rules of the Game

The goal of Wordle is to guess a secret word within six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. After each guess, the color of the letters changes to show how close your guess was to the word.

    Green: The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
    Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    Gray: The letter is not in the word in any spot.
